### **2. Visual Identity**

#### **Color Palette**

| Usage | Hex Code | CMYK / RGB | Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Deep Authority** | `#14274E` | R: 20, G: 39, B: 78 | **Primary.** Used for Logo, Headings, and Primary Buttons. |
| **Corporate Depth** | `#394867` | R: 57, G: 72, B: 103 | **Secondary.** Used for body copy, subheaders, and UI elements. |
| **Steel Accent** | `#9BA4B4` | R: 155, G: 164, B: 180 | **Accent.** Used for icons, borders, stars (reviews), and dividers. |
| **Clean Canvas** | `#F1F6F9` | R: 241, G: 246, B: 249 | **Background.** Used for main site canvas and card surfaces. |

#### **Typography**

* **Primary Heading (H1-H3):** *Inter* or *Montserrat* (Bold/700).
* *Style:* Tight letter spacing (`tracking-tighter`) for a modern, tech-focused look.


* **Body Copy:** *Inter* or *Roboto* (Regular/400).
* *Style:* 1.6x line height to ensure readability in long service descriptions.


* **Monospace (Optional):** *JetBrains Mono* for specific "AI/Code" snippets or tech specs.

---

### **3. UI & Component Rules**

* **Buttons:** * **Shape:** Sharp corners (Radius: 2px or 0px) to signify structure and discipline.
* **Primary:** `#14274E` background with `#F1F6F9` text.
* **Hover:** Shift to `#394867` with a 0.2s transition.


* **Review Cards:** * Flat design only. Use a 1px solid border of `#9BA4B4` instead of drop shadows.
* Star ratings must be `#9BA4B4` (Gray-scale stars feel more professional than yellow for agencies).


* **Iconography:** * Thin-line icons (Lucide/Feather style).
* Icon color: `#14274E` for active/high-priority, `#9BA4B4` for secondary.



---

### **4. Do’s & Don'ts**

* **Do:**
* Use generous white space (margins and padding) to keep the layout "premium."
* Use high-contrast text to ensure accessibility (WCAG AA compliant).
* Ensure all images/placeholders are high-resolution and professional.


* **Don't:**
* **No Gradients:** Keep colors flat and solid.
* **No Rounded UI:** Avoid bubbly or highly rounded corners (Radius > 8px).
* **No Accent Colors:** Do not introduce red, yellow, or green unless it's a critical status indicator (e.g., Form Error).



---

### **5. Motion & Interaction**

* **Scroll Reveal:** Elements should fade and slide up 20px when entering the viewport.
* **Micro-interactions:** Links should have a subtle underline expansion on hover using the color `#9BA4B4`.
