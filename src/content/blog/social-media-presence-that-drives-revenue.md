## Followers Don't Pay Bills — Customers Do

Most businesses approach social media completely wrong. They chase likes, follows, and viral moments while ignoring the metrics that actually matter: leads, conversions, and revenue.

At **Bykorp**, we build social media strategies that are engineered to generate business results — not just engagement.

## Bykorp's Social Media Framework

### 1. Platform Selection Strategy

Not every platform deserves your attention. Bykorp analyzes where your target audience actually spends their time and focuses resources on the platforms that deliver the highest ROI.

- **LinkedIn** — B2B lead generation and thought leadership
- **Instagram** — visual brands, lifestyle, and e-commerce
- **Facebook** — local businesses, community building, paid advertising
- **TikTok** — brands targeting Gen Z and younger millennials

### 2. Content Pillars

Bykorp develops 3-5 content pillars for every client — recurring themes that establish your brand voice and keep your content calendar consistent.

**Example content pillars for a digital agency:**
- Behind-the-scenes process content
- Client success stories and case studies
- Industry tips and educational content
- Brand culture and team highlights
- Thought leadership and hot takes

### 3. Community Management

Posting content is only half the job. Bykorp manages community engagement — responding to comments, initiating conversations, and building genuine relationships that turn followers into brand advocates.

### 4. Paid Social Advertising

Organic reach is declining on every platform. Bykorp amplifies your best-performing content with targeted paid campaigns, using lookalike audiences and retargeting to maximize conversion rates.

Bykorp provides monthly reports that go beyond vanity metrics. We track:
- **Revenue attributed to social media**
- **Cost per lead from social campaigns**
- **Engagement rate trends**
- **Audience growth quality** (not just quantity)
