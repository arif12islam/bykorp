## SEO Has Changed — Has Your Strategy?

Search engine optimization in 2026 looks nothing like it did five years ago. Google's AI-powered algorithms are smarter than ever, and the old tricks — keyword stuffing, link farms, thin content — will actively harm your rankings.

At **Bykorp**, we stay ahead of every algorithm update so our clients don't have to.

## What Works in SEO Right Now

### 1. Topical Authority Through Content Clusters

Google rewards websites that demonstrate deep expertise in specific topics. Bykorp builds content cluster strategies where a central "pillar page" links to detailed supporting articles, creating a web of topical authority.

### 2. Technical SEO Excellence

Bykorp's technical SEO audits address the invisible factors that make or break rankings:
- **Core Web Vitals** — page speed, interactivity, and visual stability
- **Structured Data (JSON-LD)** — helping Google understand your content
- **Crawlability** — ensuring every important page is indexed
- **Mobile-first indexing** — flawless mobile experiences

### 3. User Experience as a Ranking Factor

Google's helpful content system evaluates whether your pages actually satisfy user intent. Bykorp designs pages that answer the user's question completely, reducing bounce rates and increasing dwell time.

### 4. Local SEO for Service Businesses

For businesses serving specific geographic areas, Bykorp optimizes Google Business profiles, builds local citations, and creates location-specific landing pages that capture hyper-local search traffic.

### 5. E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust)

Google wants to see that real experts create your content. Bykorp helps establish your brand's E-E-A-T through author bios, credentials, original research, and authoritative backlinks.

## What No Longer Works

- **Keyword stuffing** — Google's AI can detect unnatural keyword usage
- **Buying backlinks** — risky and often penalized
- **Thin, AI-generated content** — Google devalues content without genuine expertise
- **Ignoring mobile** — mobile-first indexing means mobile IS your primary site
- **Slow websites** — speed is a confirmed ranking factor
