## Is Your Website Costing You Customers?

Your website is your digital storefront. If it looks outdated, loads slowly, or confuses visitors, you're losing money every single day. At **Bykorp**, we've audited hundreds of websites and consistently find the same critical issues holding businesses back.

Here are five warning signs that it's time to work with a professional web design agency like Bykorp.

## 1. Your Website Isn't Mobile-Friendly

Over 60% of web traffic now comes from mobile devices. If your website doesn't adapt seamlessly to phones and tablets, you're invisible to the majority of your potential customers.

**What Bykorp does differently:** Every website we design is built mobile-first. We start with the smallest screen and scale up, ensuring a flawless experience on every device.

## 2. Your Pages Take More Than 3 Seconds to Load

Google research shows that 53% of mobile users abandon a site that takes longer than 3 seconds to load. Slow websites don't just frustrate visitors — they tank your search rankings.

**Bykorp's approach:** We use Next.js with server-side rendering and static generation to achieve sub-second load times. Our sites consistently score 95+ on Google PageSpeed Insights.

## 3. Visitors Can't Find What They Need

If your bounce rate is above 60%, your navigation and content hierarchy are failing. Good web design guides visitors naturally toward the information they need and the actions you want them to take.

**How Bykorp solves this:** We apply conversion-centered design principles — clear visual hierarchy, strategic CTAs, and intuitive navigation that reduces friction at every step.

## 4. Your Website Looks Like Everyone Else's

Generic templates might save money upfront, but they make your brand forgettable. In a crowded market, a distinctive design is a competitive advantage.

**The Bykorp difference:** We create custom designs tailored to your brand identity. Every element — from typography to micro-animations — is crafted to make your business memorable.

## 5. You Can't Update Your Own Content

If making a simple text change requires calling a developer, your website is a liability. Modern businesses need websites that empower their teams, not depend on external help.

**Bykorp builds for independence:** We integrate intuitive content management systems and train your team to make updates confidently.

## Ready for a Website That Works as Hard as You Do?

If any of these signs sound familiar, it's time for a redesign. Bykorp builds websites that look stunning, load instantly, and convert visitors into customers.
